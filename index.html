Hello, my name is Shugyla!

‍ I am a QA Tester, Frontend Developer and UI/UX Designer who loves to create useful, intuitive, and engaging products.

As a curious and creative designer with a strong critical-thinking mindset, I enjoy exploring how things work and finding practical, thoughtful ways to improve people’s lives through design. I approach problems with both creativity and logic, aiming to deliver solutions that are innovative yet grounded in real user needs. With a diverse background and hands-on experience, I bring a well-rounded skill set and a thoughtful perspective to my work. I am continuously growing as a designer, refining my process, and learning from each project I take on. Feel free to explore my work below, and don’t hesitate to reach out if you have any questions or would like to collaborate.
